About Me

I'm an Android Growth Engineer at Facebook! I graduated from the University of Michigan in 2017 and have been living in Silicon Valley since then.

<-- Peep the crooked pin on my shirt.

I optimize for being a jack of all trades. I find it important to spend time learning and understanding a wide spectrum of different things. I regularly go through periods of time where I become incrediby obsessed with something. Some concrete examples include: learning to play golf, doing a 21 day vlog challenge (see more below), testing out polyphasic sleep cycles, and learning how to play guitar and piano.

Come talk to me about what the latest and greatest obsession is :)
