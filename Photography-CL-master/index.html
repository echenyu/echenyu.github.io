21 Day Vlog Challenge


On June 18th, I started a challenge to make a video a day for a month. I had a few goals in doing this:

1). I wanted to learn how to talk to a camera better.
2). I wanted to force myself to do something that made me uncomfortable.
3). I wanted to improve on my video editing skills.

Doing videos daily is REALLY hard. I set out to do 30 straight days of videos, but ended up stopping on day 22. The main reason I stopped was because there wasn't much net gain from making daily videos anymore. When I started making videos, I could see tangible improvement in every video I made. By day 22, I was repeating what I knew how to do, and I wasn't learning anymore. On July 9th, I decided to stop making daily videos for the sake of focusing on quality instead. All in all, this challenge helped me recognize that I LOVE content creation, and I'm looking forward to making more videos at a slower cadence. Check out my YouTube here!
